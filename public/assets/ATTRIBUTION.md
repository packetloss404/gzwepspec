# Asset Attribution

Weapon and part inspect renders in this folder were sourced from the Gray Zone Warfare Wiki on Fandom.

- Source: https://gray-zone-warfare.fandom.com/
- Fandom page footer states community content is available under CC-BY-SA unless otherwise noted.
- Gray Zone Warfare and its game artwork belong to MADFINGER Games.

These local copies are used for prototype UI rendering reliability instead of hotlinking Fandom CDN image URLs.

The canonical inventory of local files lives in `src/data/assetManifest.ts`. Official Gray Zone Warfare Steam store images are currently referenced remotely from that manifest and are not redistributed in this folder.
